The EEOC's review of its DEI practices introduces a regulatory and reputational topic that can matter for employees, consumers, and long term brand strength. For shareholders, these developments together frame a mix of external scrutiny and potential opportunity that could shape how Nike manages risks, invests in its operations, and positions the brand in key sports markets over time.
